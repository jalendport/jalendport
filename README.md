### Hi there 👋

I'm Jalen — a designer & web developer based in the New River Valley of Virginia.

Most of my days are spent building things with [Craft CMS](https://craftcms.com), [Laravel](https://laravel.com), [Vue](https://vuejs.org), and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com). I care a lot about workflows — maybe too much. A repetitive, clunky process is the kind of thing I'll happily disappear for an afternoon to fix, and most of what you'll find here came out of exactly that itch: smoothing rough edges so other people don't have to. ⚡

### What you'll find on my profile

**⚡ Spark** — A set of opinionated boilerplates and tooling for spinning up new projects fast.
- [`spark-craft`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-craft) & [`spark-kirby`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-kirby) — starter boilerplates for Craft CMS and Kirby
- [`spark-cli`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-cli) — a CLI to glue it all together
- [`spark-docker-images`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-docker-images) & [`spark-github-actions`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-github-actions) — the Docker images and CI workflows behind it

**🚀 Laravel Forge** — Helpers and ready-to-deploy stacks for [Laravel Forge](https://forge.laravel.com).
- [`laravel-forge-recipes`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-recipes) — a collection of recipes and scripts to enhance your Forge workflow
- [`laravel-forge-traefik`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-traefik) & [`laravel-forge-plausible`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-plausible) — ready-to-deploy Traefik and Plausible stacks for a Forge server

**🔌 Craft CMS plugins** — A whole shelf of plugins I've built over the years.
- [`craft-preparse`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-preparse) — a field type that parses Twig when an element is saved
- [`craft-lockout`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-lockout) — temporarily lock certain users out of the control panel
- [`craft-altcha`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-altcha) — a privacy-friendly, GDPR-compliant CAPTCHA alternative for your forms
- [`craft-hideadmin`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-hideadmin), [`craft-readtime`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-readtime), and [a few more](https://github.com/jalendport?tab=repositories&q=craft-)

**🛠️ Odds & ends** — [`vscode-twig`](https://github.com/jalendport/vscode-twig) for Twig language support in VS Code, my [`dotfiles`](https://github.com/jalendport/dotfiles), and a handful of other experiments.
