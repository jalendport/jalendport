### Howdy 👋

I'm Jalen, a designer & web developer who spends most days building things with [Craft CMS](https://craftcms.com), [Laravel](https://laravel.com), [Vue](https://vuejs.org), and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com). I care a lot about workflows — maybe too much. A repetitive, clunky process is the kind of thing I'll happily disappear for an afternoon to fix, and most of my open source projects came out of exactly that itch: smoothing rough edges so other people don't have to.

### What you'll find in my repos

**Twig Toolbox** — <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/vscode-twig-toolbox/develop/images/icon.png" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`vscode-twig-toolbox`](https://github.com/jalendport/vscode-twig-toolbox) brings a complete Twig 3 grammar and a real language server to VS Code, with first-class Craft CMS awareness.

**Spark** — A set of opinionated boilerplates and tooling for spinning up new projects fast.
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/spark-craft/develop/.github/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`spark-craft`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-craft) — an opinionated starter boilerplate for Craft CMS
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/spark-cli/master/.github/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`spark-cli`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-cli) — a CLI to glue it all together
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/spark-craft-lab/develop/.github/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`spark-craft-lab`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-craft-lab) — a disposable Craft runtime for smoke-testing plugins
- [`spark-docker-images`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-docker-images) & [`spark-github-actions`](https://github.com/jalendport/spark-github-actions) — the Docker images and CI workflows behind it

**Craft CMS plugins** — A whole shelf of plugins I've built over the years.
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/craft-preparse/develop/src/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`craft-preparse`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-preparse) — a field type that parses Twig when an element is saved
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/craft-metronome/develop/src/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`craft-metronome`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-metronome) — Laravel-style task scheduling: one crontab entry, everything else in PHP
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/craft-lockout/develop/src/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`craft-lockout`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-lockout) — temporarily lock certain users out of the control panel
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/craft-altcha/master/src/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`craft-altcha`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-altcha) — a privacy-friendly, GDPR-compliant CAPTCHA alternative for your forms
- <img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jalendport/craft-readtime/develop/src/icon.svg" width="20" height="20" alt=""> [`craft-readtime`](https://github.com/jalendport/craft-readtime) — estimated read times for entries, plus [a few more](https://github.com/jalendport?tab=repositories&q=craft-)

**Laravel Forge** — Helpers and ready-to-deploy stacks for [Laravel Forge](https://forge.laravel.com).
- [`laravel-forge-recipes`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-recipes) — a collection of recipes and scripts to enhance your Forge workflow
- [`laravel-forge-traefik`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-traefik) & [`laravel-forge-plausible`](https://github.com/jalendport/laravel-forge-plausible) — ready-to-deploy Traefik and Plausible stacks for a Forge server

**Odds & ends** — [`tailwindcss-fluid`](https://github.com/jalendport/tailwindcss-fluid) adds fluid `clamp()` utilities and range variants to Tailwind CSS v4, and my [`dotfiles`](https://github.com/jalendport/dotfiles) are public if you enjoy reading those.
